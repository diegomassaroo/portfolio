2021
Fase Project
BA Thesis at ISIA Urbino

The project’s purpose is to improve the speed of acquisition and cognizance of font’s words for display, even when subjected to strong lighting of an ambient. Some scientific studies have demonstrated that the use of increased weight like Medium, Semi-Bold or Bold augments legibility in adverse conditions of low contrast. The thesis’s proposal is to enhance the typeface’s weight according to the light level in the area of observation, in such a way that facilitate usage of devices.

2020
Caporetta
Superness

Caporetta is a sans serif font born during the internship in Superness, a studio in Milan. Geometric and clear shapes characterise its structure but particular contrasts and distinct terminations give it a special aspect. Caporetta is variety, inclusiveness and originality.

2020
Angustina
Self-initiated

Angustina is an elegant display typeface, it takes inspiration from classic letter styles. The typeface is named after reading the classic Italian novel “The Tartar Steppe”. I was inspired by the sickly, stoic and mysterious man who differs for his willingness to stay at Fort Bastiani. Angustina conveys distress and stinging sensation with extreme contrasts between thick and thin strokes and exasperated serifs. The alternate of hairline and bracketed serifs gives a modern and military appearance.

2019
Fukuro
Self-initiated

Fukurō recalls diurnal and nocturnal birds of prey. It instill the cutting shapes, the wings, the movement of those animals and translate them into signs. The font experiments new shapes and contrasts that give to it some Japanese aspects.

2018
Residua
ISIA Urbino project

A new typeface based on abandoned urban places. These places have a history. They are decayed, they were built in the past and then they were left to themselves. Nature has recaptured these areas. Time has worn out these places, which are composed of broken pieces, rotten wood, mouldy bricks or rusted metals. In these areas there is usually little light, the colours of such structures are black, green and brown. They remind me of a cyberpunk, decadent and apocalyptic style.
